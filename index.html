DEXFIAT Solution

We enable a decentralized community easy, reliable, fast and secure access to fiat and cryptocurrencies while generating revenue to the protocol users and enabling borderless fund transfer. We are connecting a para-chain between the fiat and crypto system.

DEXFIAT Problem & Solution

There are several viable challenges in the fintech industry and some of the challenges is what Dexfiat is up to solving. Identified challenges and opportunities include:

Problem

  • Fraudulent fiat-crypto and crypto-fiat transactions
  • Lack of decentralized fiat-crypto market
  • Quick and easy access to crypto asset.
  • Demand for low fee borderless transfer
  • Limited Fx transaction barrier
  • High fee associated with Fiat fund transfer
  • Delayed intercontinental Fund transfer
  • Fund collation platform toward project funding

Solution

Decentralize Network
  • Decentralized platform
  • Implementation of Blockchain secured
  • Borderless FIAT transfer with low fees.
  • Secure storage and FIAT to Crypto conversion and vice versa.

Problem

Centralize Network
  • High fee associated with Fiat fund transfer
  • Delayed intercontinental Fund transfer
  • How to network with other investors / like-minded people?

Solution

Decentralize Network
  • Decentralized FIAT liquidity provision
  • Profitable revenue to Liquidity providers
  • Governance
Swap & Pool

A swap and liquidity pool that allows users to swap both synthetic fiat to crypto and also crypto to crypto. The pool allow liquidity providers to earn from all the transaction carried out on Dexfiat swap

Dexfiat Multi-sig wallet

A non-custodial wallet that allows users to securely store their coin and tokens. While enabling instant withdrawal, deposit, 2- factor authentication, wallet backup and other features.

Fiat Dex

A P2P fiat section that enables a decentralized deposit and withdrawal for users

Governance

A project for the people by the people will also implement changes and upgrade by the community vote using the DFT token
